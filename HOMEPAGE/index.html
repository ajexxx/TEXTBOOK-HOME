FAQs of Previous Year Papers

Why should one go for solving previous years’ papers?
Solving the previous years’ papers is not only a good practice to check your preparedness for the exam, but will also make you experience the real-time examination.
Are the previous years’ papers enough to prepare for the recruitment exams?
No, candidates must start with preparing the topics from the syllabus first and then solve the previous years’ papers in order to judge the speed, accuracy and hence prepare better.
Where will I get the previous years’ papers?
Searching for previous years’ papers can be a tedious task since they are present all over the internet. To save our candidates a lot of time, Testbook has curated the previous years’ papers for different recruitment exams which you can find the links to above.
What should I practice more for SSC exams from the previous years’ papers?
Previous years’ papers help you throughout the subjects that are asked in SSC CGL, SSC CHSL, SSC MTS, SSC CPO exams which are Reasoning, English, Quantitative Aptitude, General Science, General Awareness, etc. A candidate may be weak in one subject while being strong in the other subjects. For the subjects you feel, little more practice is needed, make sure you solve more of them.
How many previous years’ papers are recommended for candidates to practice?
There is no set limit of how many papers you can solve. Depending upon your preparation and time you can go for solving as many papers as you can.
I am preparing for banking exams, will I get IBPS previous years’ papers on Testbook to practice from?
If you are a banking aspirant, then you have come to the right place. Testbook does have IBPS previous years’ papers along with SBI previous years’ papers too.
Can I find SSC MTS previous years’ papers on Testbook?
Yes, the SSC MTS SSC MTS previous years’ papers are available on Testbook.
Can I find the previous years’ papers for RRB NTPC and RRB Group D recruitment?
Yes, there are previous years’ papers of both RRB NTPC and RRB Group D available for our candidates to practice from.
I was looking for REET previous years’ papers. Will I get it here?
Surely. You can get REET previous years’ papers to solve and practice from.
Will the previous years’ papers be in accordance with the latest format?
It is not necessary that the previous years’ papers are of the latest format. For some exams, the current format may change while for others it might remain the same as in certain cases the exam pattern & syllabus have changed over the years.
Will the previous years’ papers tell me the exact count of questions for different subjects?
The previous years’ papers cannot really let you know the exact count of the questions for different subjects. However, from previous years’ papers, you can definitely make out the analysis so as to know which topic is asked the most.
